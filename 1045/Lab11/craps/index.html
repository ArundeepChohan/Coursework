CPSC 1045 - Craps Game

Rules:

The Game of Craps is played by a single player with two dice.
The game starts by rolling the pair of dice - from this first roll, there are 3 possible outcomes:

  1. If the total of the two dice is 7 or 11, you WIN.
  2. If the total is 2, 3, or 12, you LOSE.
  3. Otherwise, the total of the first roll is known as your point,
    and you must roll again and again until either...
    • you roll a 7 --> and LOSE, or...
    • your roll your point --> and WIN
